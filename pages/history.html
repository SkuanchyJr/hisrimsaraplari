Our History

325 Years of Legacy

The Hısrım family story is one of resilience, tradition, and deep connection to their land. For over three centuries, our family has preserved its identity, culture, and values — through times of prosperity and hardship alike.

The Origin

Roots in Mesopotamia

Our story begins in Mardin, in the heart of ancient Mesopotamia. A land where civilizations were born, where vine cultivation dates back 5,000 years. It is here that the Hısrım family laid its foundations, cultivating not only grapes but a way of life deeply intertwined with the land.

The Diaspora

Across 15 Countries

Over the centuries, members of the Hısrım family have spread across the globe — to Europe, the Americas, the Middle East, and beyond. Yet despite the distance, the bond with Mardin and with each other has never wavered. The family legacy is a thread that connects generations across borders.

15 Countries
A Journey Through Time

Timeline

1700
Chapter I

The Beginnings

The earliest records of the Hısrım family emerge from the limestone lands of ancient Mesopotamia. In the shadow of Mardin's cliffs, a family takes root — cultivating vines, weaving traditions, and building a legacy that would endure for centuries.

The name Hısrım first appears in the records of the Syriac Orthodox community of Mardin, linked to viticulture and sacred craft.
1850
Chapter II

Cultural Consolidation

The family deepens its roots in Syriac tradition. Winemaking becomes a sacred element of community life. The Hısrım artisans are known for their textiles — creating ceremonial vestments for the churches and monasteries of Tur Abdin.

Historical textiles woven by the family still survive in the collections of the Deyrülzafaran Monastery.
1915
Chapter III

Resilience Through Darkness

A period of profound upheaval reshapes the region. Despite the turmoil, the Hısrım family endures — preserving its identity, protecting its heritage, and planting the seeds of a diaspora that would span the globe.

The family's bonds prove stronger than any border. Traditions, recipes, and stories are carried across continents.
1950
Chapter IV

Across 15 Countries

The Hısrım family branches extend across the world — Turkey, Syria, Brazil, Argentina, Spain, Germany, Australia, and beyond. Yet despite the distances, the connection to Mardin remains the heartbeat of the family.

14 family branches documented across 15 countries: Helvacı, Hanaşe, Anache, Güzeliş, Özkardeşler, Gezer.
2024
Chapter V

The Renewal

A new chapter begins. The family legacy is revived through heritage preservation, cultural projects, and the continuation of ancestral traditions. The Hısrım wine returns to the land of its birth — each bottle carrying the weight of 325 years.

This website is itself part of the renewal — a living archive that grows with each family member's contribution.

A family heritage spanning 325 years. From Mardin to the world.
